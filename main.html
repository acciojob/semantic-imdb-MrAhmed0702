Plot Summary

A young hobbit named Frodo Baggins is entrusted with an ancient and powerful ring. He embarks on a perilous journey across Middle-earth to destroy it in the fires of Mount Doom. Accompanied by a fellowship of allies, he faces dark forces, temptations, and epic battles to save the world from the dark lord Sauron.
